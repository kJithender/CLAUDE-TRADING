## Core thesis

Concentrate capital in the **AI infrastructure supercycle** — specifically the picks-and-shovels
(NVDA, AVGO, AMD) and the hyperscalers demonstrating actual AI monetization (META, MSFT).
These businesses are compounding earnings at 30–85% YoY while the broader market grows 28%.
The edge is **analytical**: most retail participants and some institutional money is still
anchored to pre-AI valuation frameworks; earnings revisions for these names have consistently
lagged actual performance, creating repeated "buy the dip" entries.

Secondary theme: **AI power infrastructure** (VST, CEG). Data-center electricity demand is the
binding constraint behind AI buildout. Nuclear operators with long-term PPAs to hyperscalers
are trading at a fraction of the value they deserve.

## Exit rules

- **18% trailing stop** placed as a trailing-stop order immediately after fill. Never mental.
- **-12% midday rule**: at the midday routine, close any position >12% below entry price.
- **Thesis break**: exit within one session if the primary thesis is invalidated — e.g., NVDA
  losing a major hyperscaler, AVGO's AI revenue decelerating (not just software), META ad
  growth reversing on macro.
- **Winner pyramiding exits**: take partial profit at 2× the stop distance (i.e., ~36% gain) by
  selling 25% of the position; leave the rest to run with a tightened trailing stop.
