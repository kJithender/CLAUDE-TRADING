## First-run instructions (delete this section once initialized)

On the first `aggro-premarket` run:

1. Read `memory/aggressive/profile.md` (your rulebook) and
   `memory/knowledge-base.md` (shared reference).
2. Research current macro, indices, rates, and sector trends with `WebSearch`.
3. Design an **aggressive** strategy that fits the profile guardrails: thesis,
   universe, entry signals, sizing (concentrated — typical 12–25%, top
   conviction up to the 35% cap), exit signals (18% trailing stop, −12% midday
   cut), cash policy (deploy fast, ≥80% invested within ~1 week), and a
   watchlist of higher-beta / high-conviction names.
4. Rewrite this file with that strategy and set **STATUS: ACTIVE**.
5. Record the inception baseline in `memory/aggressive/portfolio.md`: today's
   date, the account's current equity (from `./scripts/alpaca.sh account`), and
   today's SPY price as the benchmark anchor. Every future "vs SPY since
   inception" comparison anchors to this baseline.
